Analysis of US Rent Prices and Salary

Will Stout, Xuyang Li

With a growing wealth inequality in America, it is becoming increasingly difficult to set yourself up to achieve financial stability. This visualization project seeks to create a tool that identifies the most optimal place for people to live for their specific job, and to educate them of the financial ramifications of their decisions.
This visualiztion shows the average salary of a curated set of job types across different cities in the US. Average rent and expected tax rate of the average salary can be factored in for each city and job type. And the top 3 optimal cities can be highlighted under your chosen settings.
